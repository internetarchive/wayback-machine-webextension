Welcome to the Internet Archive's
Wayback Machine Browser Extension


This extension collects and stores information about the search queries you make, and the web pages you visit, to provide you with information about those pages and to help the Wayback Machine archive the Web. In some cases, information collected by the extension may be personally identifiable, but we do not attempt to analyze any information collected by the extension to determine the identity of any user.

By enabling this extension, you agree to allow it to read and write cookies under the archive.org domain.
