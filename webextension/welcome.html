Wayback Machine Extension


This extension collects and stores information about the search queries you make and the web pages you view to provide you with information about those pages and to help the Wayback Machine archive the Web. In some cases, information collected by the extension may be personally identifiable, but we do not attempt to analyze any information collected by the extension to determine the identity of any user.
